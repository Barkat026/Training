🛠️ How to Become an Engineer

Step 1: Complete Secondary Education

Focus on subjects like Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry in high school. These provide the essential foundation for engineering studies.

Step 2: Earn a Bachelor's Degree

Enroll in an accredited engineering program such as Mechanical, Electrical, Civil, or Computer Engineering. This is your core technical education.

Step 3: Consider Further Education

Specialize further by pursuing a Master's or PhD degree if you want to work in research, academia, or advanced technical roles.

Step 4: Gain Experience

Take part in internships, entry-level jobs, or certification programs. Real-world experience is key to becoming a successful engineer.
